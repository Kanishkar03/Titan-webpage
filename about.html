Titan Watches

A Flagship Brand of Titan Company Limited

Brand Portfolio

  • Titan: The core brand, Titan, offers a wide range of watches catering to different segments, from classic analog watches to contemporary styles.
  • Fastrack: Aimed at the youth, Fastrack offers trendy and affordable watches with bold designs.
  • Sonata: Sonata is Titan’s value-for-money brand, offering durable and stylish watches at affordable prices.
  • Xylys: Positioned as a luxury brand, Xylys offers premium Swiss-made watches that combine elegance with sophisticated craftsmanship.
  • Raga: Raga is a collection exclusively designed for women, featuring elegant and stylish watches that double as jewelry pieces.
  • Nebula: Nebula offers a collection of 18k gold watches, blending the craftsmanship of traditional jewelry with the precision of watchmaking.
  • Edge: Known for its ultra-thin watches, Titan Edge is a marvel of engineering, featuring watches that are sleek and minimalistic.

Innovation and Design

Innovation: Titan has consistently introduced innovative features in its watches, such as the world’s slimmest watch, Titan Edge. The brand also ventured into the smartwatch segment with the Titan Connected series, offering features like fitness tracking and notifications.

Design: Titan collaborates with renowned designers to create unique and stylish timepieces. The brand’s design philosophy balances aesthetics with functionality.

Manufacturing Excellence

Titan’s state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Hosur, Tamil Nadu, is one of the most advanced watchmaking units globally. The facility integrates cutting-edge technology with skilled craftsmanship to produce high-quality watches.

Global Reach

Titan watches are not just popular in India but are also exported to over 30 countries. The brand’s international presence is growing, with an increasing number of retail stores and partnerships across the globe.
